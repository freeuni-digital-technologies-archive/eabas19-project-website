Aqua-Geo

Our goal is to offer the Georgian water with unique natural features to customers using the high technologies of production.

The Company’s goal is to produce and popularize the high quality unique Georgian mineral water on Georgian and foreign markets.


The plant

A water pouring plant is equipped with fully automated and modernized lines from the best European brand – for glass and PET bottling as well as packing. The lines use state-of-the-art technologies to ensure absolute product quality protection.

The lines use state-of-the-art technologies to ensure absolute product quality protection. The capacity of the plant is 20,000 bottles per hour.

The water is produced from two wells 150 m deep. Both wells are located on the production area and the water flowing from the Earth interior is supplied to the plant via stainless steel pipes. The German Company is constantly monitoring the well parameters using an EcoTech electronic logger. High quality, stability and compliance of the water with European and American standards is certified by the Georgian (Gama), German (Fresenius Institution) and Austrian (BIO4) laboratory findings.

Though the extracted natural water is of high quality and pure. in the plant the water is processed and additionally filtered by a state-of-the-art process technology of computerized monitoring. What is also important, on the site of water processing no water is accumulated in the reservoirs. Being filtered the water extracted from the wells is continuously supplied to the pouring lines.

The highly qualified operational and technical personnel, trained by French and Italian specialists, ensure the continuous and effective operation of the plant.
