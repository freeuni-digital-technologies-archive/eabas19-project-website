Certificate of Accreditaion

On February 3rd, 2015, the laboratory of "Aqua Geo" successfully passed all of the stages of the SST ISO/IEK 17025:2010 tests and got the certicifate of accreditaion.

Accreditaion is the process of recognition of the competentive states and thus the recognition by the international ISO-17025 standard. it can be labeled only as the significant success.

The fact that the plant where the natural water is produces has its own laboratory which is recognized by the international states creates the sense of liability in customers, as with the process of accreditaion it becomes clear that the results of the tests made by the laboratory are trustworth, accurate, independent and objectiveness that means the company produces the product which is allways of the best quality.
